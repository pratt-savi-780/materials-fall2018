In-class Exercise: D3.js

Part 1: SVG

  1. Remix this Glitch site
  2. In the HTML file, add a few more rect elements, changing their fill and positions (x and y).
  3. Confirm that the rectangles you add are visible on the resulting page.

Part 2: SVG with D3

  1. Remix this Glitch site
  2. In the JavaScript file, add a few more elements to the rectangles array, changing their fill and positions (x and y).
  3. Confirm that the rectangles you add are visible on the resulting page.

Part 3: maps with D3

  1. Remix this Glitch site.
  2. Experiment with changing the styles for the states in the CSS file.
  3. Experiment with changing the projection for the map.
  4. Try adding your own GeoJSON or CSV data to the map.
  5. Try adding interactivity (for example, mouseover to the map).
  6. Explore the D3 wiki gallery to get an idea of what else is possible with D3.
